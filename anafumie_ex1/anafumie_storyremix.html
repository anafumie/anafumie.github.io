A week in the past by Ana Fumie

Introduction to Story:

The year is 2068 the world is catastrophic, the zombie nation dominated the earth. After 2 years just few people still alive and walking like nomads to survive. Not only the virus was killing, but the human body couldn’t resist to the fear, angry and hungry.
My survivor group finally had all the technology to fix a time machine to send me when everything started and try to save the human future. The only problem is I will have only one week in the past to do it.
Now I’m in San Francisco and the year is 2066, I have all the research and hope in my hands. remixes of this website. When you finish drag this file into a browser like Chrome to see what it looks like as a web page.
